✦ Our Story

A Family Tradition, Baked With Heart

Sunrise Bakery started as a dream in Priya Sharma's home kitchen in Guwahati, 2008. What began with one sourdough loaf has grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousands of families.

  • All breads are slow-fermented for a minimum of 18 hours for depth of flavour
  • We source flour from local mills in Assam and never use artificial preservatives
  • Every cake is custom-made to your occasion — no two are ever the same
  • Our team of 6 bakers starts work at 3 AM so your morning is perfect
